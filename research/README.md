# LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS — Research Repository

This repository collects high-signal content from practitioners who actively work on LinkedIn growth, B2B content, demand generation, and related go-to-market systems.

The goal is to build a research base strong enough to support a practical LinkedIn organic content playbook for B2B SaaS.

---

## Topic

**LinkedIn organic content strategy for B2B SaaS**

This research focuses on how B2B SaaS teams and creators use LinkedIn to:
- build audience and authority,
- create demand,
- support pipeline,
- and turn content into a repeatable growth system.

---

## What is inside this repo

- **`/research/sources.md`**  
  Master list of all selected experts, with links, dates, and brief annotations.

- **`/research/linkedin-posts/`**  
  LinkedIn posts collected by author, organized into separate files.

- **`/research/youtube-transcripts/`**  
  YouTube transcripts summaries organized by video and author.

- **`/research/other/`**  
  Any extra materials, notes, screenshots, or supporting documents.

---

## Why these experts were chosen

The experts in this repository were selected because they are practitioners, not just commentators.

They were chosen for:
- real-world experience,
- repeated publishing around content, growth, or GTM,
- strong opinions that can be turned into strategic patterns,
- and relevance to B2B SaaS content, distribution, and demand creation.

The goal is not volume. The goal is signal.

---

## How the research is organized

Each source is documented with:
- title,
- platform,
- date,
- link,
- and a short note on why it matters.

Each collected post or transcript is saved in a separate file so the material can later be reused for:
- pattern analysis,
- content strategy development,
- and playbook creation.
