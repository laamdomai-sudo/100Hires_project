## Post 1:

Title: Marketing KPIs Must Align With Business Results
URL: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/chriswalker171_finance-marketing-b2b-activity-7254121607781076992-K4Ag/
Date: 2025

### Summary

In this post, Chris Walker argues that the root cause of most B2B pipeline problems is not creativity or content volume, but misaligned marketing KPIs.
He says that many B2B teams still optimize for leads, traffic, and influenced revenue, while failing to measure what actually matters: pipeline, ROI, new logo efficiency, and growth rate.

The core idea:
👉 Marketing strategy is shaped by measurement, so if the KPIs are wrong, the strategy will be wrong too.

### Key Insights

- Pipeline is the real problem for many B2B companies.
- Marketing KPIs should be aligned with business results.
- Leads and traffic are not enough to judge marketing success.
- Attribution should be a secondary tool, not the primary target.
- Better measurement creates more creative and effective marketing.
- Marketing should be accountable to pipeline, ROI, and growth efficiency.

### Why it matters

This post is important for B2B SaaS because it shifts the focus from activity metrics to business outcomes.

For a playbook, the takeaway is:
- measure what drives revenue,
- stop optimizing for vanity metrics,
- and design marketing around unit economics.

### How to use this in the playbook

- Replace lead-based success metrics with pipeline and ROI targets.
- Align marketing planning with growth and finance goals.
- Treat attribution as supporting data, not the core strategy.
- Build campaigns that are accountable to business impact.
